About TCG

Technical Consulting Group (TCG) is an international technical consultancy delivering engineering, defense, and industrial advisory services for complex and mission-critical projects.

TCG supports public institutions, defense organizations, and private companies across defense & security, aerospace, infrastructure, energy, and industrial systems. We provide end-to-end technical consulting—from system planning and design to implementation, supervision, and operational support.

With a strong focus on technical accuracy, operational efficiency, and international standards, TCG acts as an independent bridge between clients, manufacturers, and contractors, ensuring reliable, compliant, and sustainable solutions.

Our mission is to help organizations execute high-value technical projects with confidence, precision, and long-term reliability.
